# Jobarium User Personas (MVP)

## Purpose
Define primary user personas to align product decisions, UX flows, and prioritization.

## Persona 1: SMB Hiring Manager (Primary Buyer/User)

- Role:
  - Owner, operations manager, or office manager hiring for frontline roles.
- Company profile:
  - Small to mid-sized business with frequent recurring hires.
- Main goals:
  - Fill roles quickly with less screening overhead.
  - Avoid wasting interviews on candidates with hidden dealbreakers.
- Main pain points:
  - Too many low-signal applications.
  - Repetitive first-call screening.
  - Inconsistent candidate comparison across team members.
- Success definition:
  - Gets interview-ready packets with high fit in days, not weeks.
- Product needs:
  - role templates
  - automation controls
  - packet-first inbox
  - authenticity trust signals

## Persona 2: Recruiter / HR Generalist (Primary Workflow Operator)
- Role:
  - In-house recruiter or HR generalist running multiple open roles.
- Main goals:
  - Standardize screening and reduce manual outreach.
  - Improve conversion from screening to interview.
- Main pain points:
  - Context switching between tools.
  - Noisy candidate pipelines.
  - Low visibility into why ranked candidates were surfaced.
- Success definition:
  - Higher packet-to-interview conversion with less manual effort.
- Product needs:
  - explainable fit breakdown
  - one-click actions (interview/clarify/reject)
  - integration-friendly notifications and exports

## Persona 3: Skilled Trade Candidate (Primary Supply User)
- Role:
  - HVAC, electrician, plumbing, maintenance technician.
- Main goals:
  - Be found for relevant jobs without repetitive applications.
  - Spend less time applying and more time proving fit.
- Main pain points:
  - Re-entering profile data across job platforms.
  - Getting buried in large applicant queues.
  - Unclear feedback on rejections.
- Success definition:
  - Receives relevant invites and converts to interviews faster.
- Product needs:
  - CV import and smart prefill
  - short onboarding with clear value
  - mobile-first Q&A completion flow

## Persona 4: Skilled Tech-Savvy Professional Candidate
- Role:
  - Software engineer, data analyst, product designer, or similar digital professional.
- Main goals:
  - Discover high-fit opportunities quickly with minimal friction.
  - Understand why roles match and optimize profile visibility.
- Main pain points:
  - Low signal in generic job feeds.
  - Repetitive applications despite already having strong online profiles.
  - Distrust of opaque ranking systems.
- Success definition:
  - Receives relevant invites with transparent match reasoning and fast turnaround.
- Product needs:
  - import from external profiles and resume
  - explainable match breakdown
  - richer role filters and preference controls

## Persona 5: Mid-Level, Mid-Savvy Professional Candidate
- Role:
  - Candidate with 4-10 years of experience in operations, support, coordination, or mixed digital/frontline roles.
- Main goals:
  - Find stable opportunities without spending hours on repetitive forms.
  - Get clear guidance while completing profile and Q&A steps.
- Main pain points:
  - Confusing application workflows and too many portals.
  - Unclear required fields and low confidence in profile quality.
  - Drop-off when process feels long or overly technical.
- Success definition:
  - Completes onboarding easily, receives understandable invites, and can respond confidently.
- Product needs:
  - simple guided onboarding with clear progress
  - plain-language instructions and fewer decisions per step
  - mobile-friendly, low-friction Q&A flow

## Persona 6: Platform Admin / Support Operator (Internal)
- Role:
  - Internal operations role handling support, quality control, and policy workflows.
- Main goals:
  - Ensure system reliability and policy compliance.
  - Resolve disputes and failed automation quickly.
- Main pain points:
  - Troubleshooting without enough observability.
  - Managing GDPR requests and authenticity disputes.
- Success definition:
  - Fast issue resolution, low incident recurrence, auditable actions.
- Product needs:
  - admin audit logs
  - event replay/retry tools
  - review workflow for authenticity appeals

## Persona Prioritization (MVP)
- Tier 1:
  - SMB Hiring Manager
  - Recruiter / HR Generalist
  - Skilled Trade Candidate
- Tier 2:
  - Skilled Tech-Savvy Professional Candidate
  - Mid-Level, Mid-Savvy Professional Candidate
- Tier 3:
  - Platform Admin / Support Operator

## Key Product Implications
- Reduce setup time for employers with templates and defaults.
- Keep candidate onboarding short and confidence-building across different digital comfort levels.
- Make ranking explainable and actionable for HR operators.
- Provide both power-user controls and guided defaults in candidate experience.
- Build baseline admin tools early enough for pilot reliability.
