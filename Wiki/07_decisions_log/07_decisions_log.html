DEC-0006: Provider strategy for MVP integrations

  • Date: 2026-02-12
  • Status: Accepted
  • Owner: CTO
  • Context: Needed explicit direction on provider abstraction depth.
  • Decision: Use direct provider SDKs behind thin internal adapters for email/SMS/payments.
